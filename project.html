What is NFT?

Types of persons in NFT Games

Normal Investor

People who invest, play their own account and earn 100% of the profit

Managers

Big investors with multiple accounts who let other people play for them. They're the ones who will decide how much the sharing profit is gonna be.

Scholars

People who play for the managers without investing even one cent. They just grind in order to earn some % of the profit.
